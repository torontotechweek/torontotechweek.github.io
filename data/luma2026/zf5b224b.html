About Event

​Join Airess and Rootly AI for an evening of movement and connection during Toronto Tech Week - a yoga session followed by networking designed to spark curiosity and conversation. No experience needed for either.

This one's for the ambitious women building in tech. Come as you are, leave feeling grounded.
When: May 27 · 6:30–8:30pm
Where: Rootly HQ, Toronto
Spots are limited. Grab yours before they're gone.

Evening:
6:30pm — Doors open, settle in
6:45pm — Yoga session
8:15pm — Mingle & wind down
8:30pm — Wrap up

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com”
