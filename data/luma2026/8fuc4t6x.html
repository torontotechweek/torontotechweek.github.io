About Event

​Join us for an evening dedicated to the intersection of AI-driven incident management and secure, zero-trust networking. Whether you’re an SRE, a security engineer, or a founder building the next great Canadian unicorn, this is your chance to see how the industry’s best are automating resilience and simplifying connectivity.

​Agenda:

  • ​5:00 PM: Networking, food, and drinks

  • ​6:00 PM: Demos

  • ​7:00 PM: Networking, food, and drinks

  • ​8:00 PM: End of event

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.

​About Rootly AI

​Rootly AI is the AI-native on-call and incident response platform that provides proactive support to help Site Reliability Engineers resolve incidents faster, improve system resilience, and streamline on-call operations. It’s your always-on SRE copilot that automates root cause analysis and identifies patterns that drive continuous improvement—trusted by hundreds of companies like LinkedIn, NVIDIA, Replit, Elastic, Canva, Clay, Tripadvisor, and Shell.

​About Tailscale

​Tailscale is the platform for secure, identity-first connectivity, delivering private, seamless connectivity tailored for modern applications, teams, and workflows. Designed to simplify complex network architectures, Tailscale replaces outdated approaches with fast, reliable connections that boost both productivity and security.

​About PostHog

​PostHog is here to help product engineers build successful products. Every piece of SaaS that a product engineer needs, including tools for building products, talking to customers, and making sense of all your customer data, is now in one place. Put simply, we're a single platform for people who build things.
