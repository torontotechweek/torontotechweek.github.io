About Event

​Join us for an evening of connection at the Sago Mini studio in Toronto! Meet some of the creative minds behind designing digital products for kids and learn about how Sago Mini bring tech and kids together through design.

​This in-person meetup will include:

  • ​Introduction to Sago Mini 

  • ​Panel discussion

  • ​Product demonstrations (bring yours!)

  • ​Social time to chat + connect

​If you are a maker, thinker, and doer who operates at the intersection of Design, Tech, and Kids, this event is for you. Consider this a warm welcome! We're excited to give you an exclusive, behind-the-scenes look at Sago Mini and bring some creative minds together. 

​

​Hosted by Sago Mini, this event will include insights from:

​Jason Krogh - Founder and GM, Sago Mini

​Stephanie Lemoine - Sr. Director of Design, Sago Mini

​Colin McCune -  Sr. Director of Engineering, Sago Mini 

​Amy Weinrieb - Product Manager, Sago Mini 

​Produced by Nat Skillen - Sago Mini + Spin Master Digital Games

​⭐ Agenda ⭐

​5:30 – 6:00 PM | Arrival + Welcome

​6:00 – 6:30 PM | Introduction to Sago Mini 

​6:30 – 7:30 PM | Panel Discussion

​7:30 – 9:00 PM | Open Social Mixer

​​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
