About Event

​What happens when frontline clinical reality meets AI innovation, national leadership, and global collaboration?

​Anchoram Canada is pleased to host an executive roundtable featuring Dr. Simon Kos, Global Chief Medical Officer at Heidi Health, and Dr. Rashaad Bhyat, Senior Clinical Leader at Canada Health Infoway, for a focused discussion on the future of AI in healthcare.

​Bringing together perspectives from Australia and Canada, this session will explore how leading global practices can be translated into scalable impact within the Canadian healthcare system.

​Dr. Kos shares the perspective of a physician leader who has practiced critical care medicine in Australia and led global digital health initiatives at Microsoft and other international organizations over the past two decades.
Dr. Bhyat offers a national view, advancing digital health adoption across Canada and shaping interoperability, clinician engagement, and system-wide innovation.

​Among our discussion topics, we’ll explore:

  • ​How AI is moving from pilot to real-world clinical impact

  • ​What it takes to drive clinician adoption at scale

  • ​Patient impact of AI

  • ​The role of interoperability, data quality, and workflow integration

  • ​How global lessons from Australia can be applied in Canada

​Designed for senior clinical, technology and policy leaders, this executive roundtable focuses on moving beyond experimentation to measurable transformation.

​Space is limited and there will be a waitlist for this highly anticipated event.

​Expect a dynamic exchange of practical insights and peer perspectives, centered on one key question:

​How do we turn global AI innovation into everyday clinical reality for clinicians and patients in Canada?
