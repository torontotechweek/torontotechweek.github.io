About Event

​What if you could build a working AI tool for your finance team — in under two hours?

​Join Float, Replit, and Campfire at the Float office during Toronto Tech Week for a hands-on hackathon where finance and accounting professionals stop talking about AI and actually build with it.

​You'll be grouped into small teams and guided by AI experts from Replit to prototype a real workflow tool — something you'd actually want to use. Live APIs, real outputs, no slides, no theory. Just rapid building, genuine collaboration, and a new understanding of what's possible.

​This is your chance to see how accessible building with AI really is — and walk away with something tangible to bring back to your team.

​How it works: Teams have 1–2 hours to design and build an AI-powered tool for finance or accounting — automating a workflow, surfacing insights, or solving a problem you've been living with. We guide the build. You bring the ideas.

Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 26–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
