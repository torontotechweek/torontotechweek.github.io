About Event

​Hear from the leaders shaping healthcare at one of North America’s fastest-growing healthtech communities.

​​Join for a night of mingling with the change-makers of healthcare (founders, operators & investors), and hear candid stories of journeys, lessons, and experiences rarely shared out loud.

​Tom Masterson is the Chief Operating Officer at Deep Genomics. Tom leverages his experience in health strategy, research, and medical devices to bring structure and continuity to the company. He oversees the Project Management Office, governance, and executive operations of the company.

​Ricky Mehra is the General Partner of Continuum Health Ventures based in Toronto and the Bay Area. He invests in early stage deep healthtech and the longevity space. Rickly is also part of the team at NextMed.Health a global conference bringing together people connected to the future of healthcare.

​Bettina Xue Griffin is a corporate lawyer at Fasken with the firm's Emerging Technology and Venture Capital group. She's passionate about supporting innovative companies scale and frequently advices founders, scale-ups and investors in the healthcare and life sciences.
