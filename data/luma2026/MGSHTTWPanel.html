​About Toronto Tech Week

​“Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com”

​About Soho House Toronto

​Set over three floors in a restored Georgian building dating back to the 1830s, Soho House Toronto is positioned at the heart of the city’s historic Bishop’s Block neighbourhood.

​​Housed in a building that once played host to one of Toronto’s first hotels, the club features a rooftop bar with views of downtown and the islands, a members-only restaurant, music and events programming, and a collection of contemporary art.
