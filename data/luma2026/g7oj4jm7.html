About Event

​Join Esplanade Ventures, Pender Ventures and Amplify Capital for an afternoon of real healthtech conversations at Luma Restaurant during Toronto Tech Week. We're putting founders, investors, and the actual buyers in the same room, the decision-makers who evaluate, champion, and ultimately sign off on healthtech deals, talking candidly about what moves things forward and what doesn't.

​2–4pm. Drinks included. Come through.

​Sponsored by MNP:

​MNP is a Canadian professional services firm providing client-focused accounting, consulting, tax, and digital services. National in scope and local in focus, MNP supports clients in more than 150 communities from coast to coast.

​​About Amplify Capital:

​Founded in 2016, Amplify Capital is an early-stage venture fund focused on backing companies using transformational technology to solve today’s biggest problems. Headquartered in Toronto and with offices in Montreal, Amplify Capital manages 3 funds, $100M in assets and specializes in Climate and Health solutions. We leverage impact-led, differentiated diligence, deep sector expertise, and a founder-first approach to deliver superior returns.

​​About Esplanade Ventures:

​Esplanade Ventures is a venture capital firm focused on digital health. Based in Toronto, with offices in Montreal, the fund invests globally and actively partners with early-stage and high growth digital health companies shaping the future of healthcare delivery. Esplanade has deep entrepreneurial, investment, and industry experience and provides its portfolio company founders with privileged access to the firm's partners, experts, and broader global ecosystem. Learn more at esplanadeventures.com.

​​About Pender Ventures:

​​Pender Ventures is a venture capital investor focused on health tech and B2B technology companies at the inflection point between commercialization and scale. As a hands-on, high-conviction investor, Pender Ventures partners closely with founders to accelerate growth and build category-leading businesses. Learn more at penderventures.com.
