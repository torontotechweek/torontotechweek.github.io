​Join us for our yearly Founder Sports Toronto Tech Week Basketball event.

We all know that networking events suck.

​Our goal is to bring founders together in a relaxed and informal setting.

​Build bonds by breaking ankles, and build long-term partnerships to make Toronto a world-class city.

Find your next customers or investors here!

  • ​Team size depends on the number of RSVPs

  • ​All skill levels welcome

  • ​Priority to founders, investors, or generally cool people in tech

  • ​​Bring basketball or running shoes

  • ​Coffee or food after the event

​Note that this is an outdoor court.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Learn more at Torontotechweek.com.
