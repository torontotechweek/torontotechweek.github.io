About Event

​
Every successful ecommerce brand begins with a strong story. The brands that break through scale are the ones that translate that story into infrastructure, systems, and revenue momentum.

​This session explores what actually changes inside a company when growth becomes complex and technology starts to shape competitive advantage.

​Hosted by JC+CO Growth Accelerants, a Shopify Plus studio working with scaling consumer brands across platform architecture, performance strategy, and AI informed growth systems, this conversation brings together operators who have navigated real inflection points on the path from traction to scale.

​Rather than theory, we will focus on what founders and leadership teams experience behind the scenes as their brands move into the next stage of maturity.

​From Story to Revenue Engine

​How strong brand narratives evolve into structured acquisition, retention, and conversion systems that support long term growth.

​Technology as a Growth Multiplier

​What changes when Shopify infrastructure, integrations, and data begin driving commercial decisions instead of reacting to them.

​The Operator’s Shift

​How leadership priorities evolve when brands move beyond early traction and begin building durable market position.

​Scaling Without Losing Identity

​How high growth brands protect what makes them distinctive while expanding across channels, markets, and customer segments.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.

​Our speakers will go in depth and share the pivotal chapters of their brand evolution moving from the first viral spark to the high-stakes challenges of building a household name.
