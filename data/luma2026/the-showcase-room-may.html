About Event

​The Showcase Room: AI / Built for Non-IT Founders.

​Two-part evening: 10 non-IT businesses showcase, then a 30-seat AI working lab. Toronto, May 27. Part of Toronto Tech Week 2026.

​🧠 A curated night for Non-IT businesses done with business card theatre.

​You’ve been to the networking events.
You know how they go.

​You walk in → collect cards → give cards → repeat the same conversation seven times → go home → follow up with two people → one replies.

​Three weeks later… you’re not sure why you went.

​This isn’t that.

​🪑 Welcome to The Showcase Room

​A curated event for Canadian businesses running at least 3+ years who are done with surface-level networking.

​You don’t walk in as a name on a badge.
You walk in with:

  • ​Your business materials

  • ​A roll-up banner

  • ​A 1-minute stage pitch

​…in front of a room that’s actually paying attention.

​⚙️ Then we build

​The second half of the night is the AI for SMBs Lab (SMB - Small Medium Business). This is a working session for business owners who want to walk out with a real solution, not a list of tools they’ll never use.

​Bring:

  • ​The operational headache

  • ​The marketing bottleneck

  • ​The sales process held together with tape

​We’ll work through it live.

​👉 You leave with something working
👉 Not something theoretical

​Bring a laptop. Bring a real problem.

​🔄 And the conversation doesn’t stop

​The 10 showcase tables stay up through the Lab.
So does the conversation.

​This is the first pilot of a planned recurring series:
Village Hive × Segmentide × The AI Collective Canada x Toronto Tech Week 2026

​The room is small on purpose.
The same businesses come back.

​That’s how real collaboration compounds.

​🗓️ Run of Show

​3:00 PM — Showcase setup (showcase businesses only)
4:00 PM — Doors open, walk the room, meet the showcase
4:30 PM — Stage pitches (10 × 1-minute)
4:45 PM — AI for SMBs Lab begins
6:00 PM — Post-Lab networking (tables still live)
7:00 PM — Wrap

​🎟️ Two Ways to Join

​🧾 Business Showcase Table — $50

​For Canadian businesses running 3+ years

​Includes:

  • ​Table + chair

  • ​Space for one roll-up banner

  • ​1-minute stage pitch

  • ​Table stays live through Lab + networking

​⚠️ 10 spots only — application required

​💡 AI for SMBs Lab Attendee — $10

​For founders and business owners who want to bring a real problem and walk out with a working AI-driven solution.

​🎟️ 30 spots — first come, first served

​Total capacity: 40

​👥 Who This Is For

​Non-IT founders.

​Caterers. Retailers. Agency owners. Consultants. Accountants. Product + service businesses.

​People with 3+ years on the clock and the scars to prove it.

​People curious about AI, but tired of being talked at about it.

​The Founders Loop

​The Founders Loop is a Toronto community for founders running businesses outside the digital services lane. Established or just starting. A room for the work, the loop, and the technology layer most founders never had time to add.

​The Village Hive Eglinton

​The Village Hive Eglinton has everything you need for a productive work day, from coworking to private offices and meeting space. We offer bright, versatile, furnished private offices for individuals/teams or single workstations in our coworking area with many extra amenities and services to help businesses succeed. Our focus is to build a collaborative community of entrepreneurs and remote workers who share space and support each other to thrive.

Located in the heart of midtown Toronto, a short distance from TTC, fitness centres, restaurants, coffee shops and more. Our coworking space and furnished offices are available on a monthly or on demand basis. Contact our community manager to help you find the right workspace option!
