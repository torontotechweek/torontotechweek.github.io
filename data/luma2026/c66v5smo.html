​

Most teams do not fail because they lack talent.
They fail because they start building before they are aligned.

​Ideas move into development too early. Feedback arrives too late. Halfway through delivery everyone realises they had a different vision of the outcome.

​The result is predictable: rework, delays, frustrated stakeholders, and wasted budget.

​This hands-on workshop during Toronto Tech Week shows how modern teams solve that problem using Miro’s AI-powered prototyping capabilities.

​Hosted by Generation Digital, a workplace AI consultancy working with organisations across the UK and Canada, this interactive session will demonstrate how teams can go from a rough concept to a working clickable prototype in minutes.

​You will also hear from Michelle Johannessen, Group Product Marketing Manager at Miro, who will share insights into how teams are using Miro’s latest AI capabilities to accelerate product thinking, collaboration, and innovation.

​Rather than theory, you will see the process happen live.

​In this workshop you will learn:

​• How AI-powered prototyping helps teams visualise ideas faster
• How early prototypes dramatically reduce costly development rework
• How product, marketing, and operations teams can collaborate around a shared visual concept
• How Miro’s AI capabilities accelerate innovation and improve decision making

​By the end of the session you will see how quickly an idea can become something tangible that teams can test, critique, and refine before development begins.

​Who should attend

​This session is designed for leaders and teams responsible for:

​• Product development
• Digital transformation
• Innovation and service design
• Operational improvement

​If your organisation wants faster alignment, clearer communication, and more confident decision making before projects begin, this workshop will show how AI-powered prototyping helps teams move from idea to execution far more efficiently.

​Seats are limited to keep the session practical and interactive.
