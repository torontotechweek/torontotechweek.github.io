About Event

​Startup Standup by Business!GO 🚀— a comedy show about building, failing & making money.

Building a startup is stressful.
Fundraising is weird.
And let’s be honest — half of it feels like a joke anyway.

So we made it one.
_______________________________________________

​This is a stand-up show for:
 💜 founders
 💜 builders
 💜 investors
 💜 and anyone trying to create something from scratch

We’re bringing real startup stories on stage — the wins, the failures, the chaos — all turned into comedy.

But it’s not just a show.

During the standup, we’re opening the room for high-quality networking. No awkward small talk. No random business cards. Fun engagement and real conversations with people who are actually building things.

Expect:
🔥 standup about startup life (yes, it’s painfully relatable)
🔥 a room full of ambitious, slightly unhinged, driven people
🔥 connections that actually make sense

If you’re building something — you’ll feel at home.
If you’re tired — you’ll laugh.
If you’re serious — you’ll meet your people.

Come for the show.
Stay for the people. And for Business!GO 😉
