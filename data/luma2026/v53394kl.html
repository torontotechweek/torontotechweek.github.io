About Event

​Brightspark and Innovobot, are hosting a relaxed smoothie meetup for founders, VCs, and the tech community. The event will be the perfect opportunity to connect and exchange insights on the venture and tech landscapes.


Brightspark Ventures is a top performing Canadian early-stage venture firm, actively investing in exceptional entrepreneurs since 1999. They are known to partner early with founders, and to provide capital and support for the long run. Brightspark manages over $500M across traditional VC Funds, as well as under a syndicate investment model open to individual and non-institutional investors.

​Innovobot is an investment platform that creates value through innovation. Our vision is to foster innovation across industries for the benefit of society. Innovobot helps companies mitigate risk and accelerate time to market by providing innovation and key commercial support in order to scale. Innovobot Resonance Ventures (IRV), our venture capital arm, invests in entrepreneurs that build pioneering technology startups for the benefit of humanity.

​In collaboration with Toronto Tech Week

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
