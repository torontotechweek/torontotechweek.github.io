About Event

​Phoenix and Toronto Ruby presents: Deep Dive into the Solid Gems with Rails.

In this talk, we'll explore how Rails' database-backed job queues, caching, and websockets work. We'll see how to use them, then explore the internals to see how they work behind-the-scenes.

Who is this event for?
For Ruby on Rails developers ready to supercharge their apps with the latest Solid Gems.

​About Phoenix:

​​We are Phoenix, a digital healthcare platform for men. We are dedicated to improving the relationship men have with their health, while building the future of patient-centric healthcare in Canada from diagnosing to delivery. Backed by world-class investors, including Y Combinator, we’ve quickly become a market leader. Based in Toronto, our team of top marketers, engineers, product managers, business leaders, and pharmacists is building the future of digital healthcare.
