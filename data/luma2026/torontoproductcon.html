About Event

​The Toronto Product Conference is a full-day, in-person conference for product managers, product leaders, and builders who want to sharpen their craft and connect with peers who take product work seriously.

​Hosted by the Toronto Product Management Association, this conference brings together product professionals from across the GTA for practical talks, honest conversations, and real-world lessons you can apply right away.

Featured Speakers:

  • ​April Dunford — CEO, Ambient Strategy, Author of Obviously Awesome

  • ​Ben Erez — Co-founder, Insider Loops; Host, Supra Insider Podcast

  • ​Ben Yoskovitz — Founding Partner, Highline Beta

  • ​Colin Matthews — Founder, Tech for Product

  • ​John Cutler — Head of Product, Dotwork

  • ​Marc Baselga — Founder, Supra

  • ​Radhika Dutt — Author, Radical Product Thinking

  • ​30+ speakers total - see more here.

​Who it’s for

  • ​Product Managers and Senior PMs

  • ​Product Leaders and aspiring leaders

  • ​Anyone serious about improving how products are built and led

​Expect a carefully curated mix of:

  • ​Insightful talks from experienced product leaders and practitioners

  • ​Tactical sessions on strategy, execution, AI, and career growth

  • ​Unconference: Interactive formats that go beyond passive listening

  • ​Time and space to meet other product people facing similar challenges

​Learn more at: tpma.ca/conference/toronto-product-conference
