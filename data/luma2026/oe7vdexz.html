About Event

​Join Lazer for an evening dedicated to the builders, engineers, and founders defining what frontier agents look like in 2026.

The Vibe: No panels. No pitch decks. Just live code, high-signal conversations, a premium open bar, and full catering.

The Schedule

  • ​6:00 PM | 🥂 Doors Open & Mingling Arrive early for the open bar and light bites. This is your time to connect with the founders and engineers who are actually shipping.

  • ​6:30 PM | 🏠 Inside the Studio (The Lazer Showcase) We’re opening the hood. We’ll showcase internal agentic workflows that help us ship 10x faster and real-world client projects where AI agents are solving high-stakes problems. We’ll also share our best practices for AI-augmented coding.

  • ​7:15 PM | 💰 The Investor’s Lens (Fireside Chat) We’re sitting down with a guest investor to cut through the hype. We’ll discuss the future of venture capital in an agent-first world and which AI trends are signal vs. noise.

  • ​7:45 PM | 🍽️ Dinner & Drinks Break A full catering spread and open bar. Refuel and network before the community demos begin.

  • ​8:15 PM | 👀 Community Demo Stage The floor belongs to the makers. We’ve selected 3-4 builders from the Toronto community to show off the agentic tools they’re pushing to the limit.

  • ​9:00 PM | 🍸 After Hours Stick around for cocktails and the kind of deep-dive conversations that only happen when the right people are in the same room.

​

Call for Demos 🚀

Are you building something agentic? We have 1-2 slots left for the Community Showcase. We want to see working code, not slides. If you want to demo your project to a room of 100+ AI builders and investors, fill out this google form to apply: https://forms.gle/hSYnFSVdnWe7DBJV8

Location: Downtown Toronto (TBA to confirmed guests)
