About Event

​​It’s about time Boardy threw another party for his friends…but this time with his friends at Jagger and Osler.

Welcome back to Toronto Tech Week’s most stylish after hours. Step into a hidden Japanese speakeasy vibe for a low-key, intimate affair. Boardy is curating the guest list, ensuring only the best people at Tech Week make the cut. Expect DJs spinning vinyl all night long on a killer sound system, alongside an extensive selection of Japanese whisky, tasty snacks, and maybe even a genuine conversation with a new friend.

​The guest list? Boardy’s managing it to make sure it’s good vibes only. If he's not your bestie already just send him a message and hop on a call to secure your invite: https://whatsapp.boardy.ai/secretafterhours

This is where Toronto Tech Week winds down properly.

​Jagger connects the most talented people in tech with their next opportunity - See more at www.hellojagger.com

Thank you to our friends at Jagger and Osler!

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 26–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
