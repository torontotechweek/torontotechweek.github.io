About Event

​

​Build with the right people. Grow with intention.

​Join us for Co-Founder Connect, presented by the Black Entrepreneurship Alliance in partnership with YSpace York University, an evening designed to help tech founders, operators, and builders find the right partners to form or strengthen their startup teams.

​Co-Founder Connect is a curated networking experience that creates intentional space for meaningful conversations, genuine connections, and long-term collaboration. Whether you are looking for technical expertise, business strategy support, or a complementary co-founder to help take your startup to the next level, this is your chance to pitch, connect, and build robust partnerships in a community-driven environment.

​Building the right team early matters. Research consistently shows that diverse and complementary teams outperform by making better decisions, innovating faster, and achieving stronger business outcomes.

​

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com

​What to Expect at Co-Founder Connect

  • ​Fireside panel on finding the perfect co-founder & team

  • ​Quickfire 60-second founder and venture pitches

  • ​Match Making Zone (Curated co-founder/team matching)

  • ​Speed Matching Zone

  • ​Professional Headshots

  • ​Interactive Zones

  • ​Networking / Mixer

  • ​Great food, drinks, and vibes

​Who Should Attend

  • ​Early-stage founders and solopreneurs

  • ​Technical co-founders and software engineers

  • ​Product designers

  • ​Fractional executives C-suite

  • ​Operators across finance, marketing, HR, and operations

  • ​Builders looking to join or support early-stage teams

​​About Black Entrepreneurship Alliance (BEA):

​
The Black Entrepreneurship Alliance (BEA) is a Black-led initiative that exists to amplify and advance the next generation of Black entrepreneurs. BEA provides fully funded programs, partnerships, and access to help Black entrepreneurs, professionals, and youth launch and grow successful ventures and careers. Learn more at www.thebea.co.

​About YSpace, York University

​YSpace is York University’s pan-university entrepreneurship and innovation hub supporting startups and entrepreneurs from a variety of sectors and communities. Our programming spans across scaling innovative technologies through customer and investor capital to scaling agri-food businesses into mass retail to diversity-focused initiatives like ELLA for women-led businesses and in partnership with Black Entrepreneurship Alliance (BEA) for Black-led businesses. We are also home to two physical hubs in the City of Markham and the Town of Georgina.

​https://www.yorku.ca/yspace/

​About Idea Mississauga

​IDEA Mississauga has the essential building blocks that entrepreneurs and innovators need to get started and grow. Through our IDEA Square One Entrepreneurship Hub, you can access a network of mentorship, partners, talent and connections to test, scale and commercialize solutions to global challenges.

​https://www.ideamississauga.ca/

​About Futurpreneur:

​​Since 1996, Futurpreneur has fostered the entrepreneurial passions of Canada’s diverse startup founders ages 18-39. We are the only national non-profit organization that provides young aspiring entrepreneurs across Canada with the loan financing, mentorship and resources they need to start or buy a business.

​ https://futurpreneur.ca/en/

​About SmartTo:

​SmartTO (Technologies of Ontario) is YSpace, York University's provincially funded mobility hub and part of the York Region's Regional Technology Development Site (RTDS), funded by the Ontario Vehicle Innovation Network (OVIN).

​Smart Mobility Applied Research and Testing-Technologies of Ontario (SmartTO) empowers startups and SMEs to develop, test and prototype advanced automotive technologies and smart mobility solutions through accessing our state of the-art equipment, innovative labs and research centres, world-renowned expertise, and concierge-style business advisory and technical services. We focus on Smart Materials and Light-weighting along with Smart Mobility, Connectivity and Reliability.

​https://www.yorku.ca/yspace/programs-streams/smartto/

​​​Code of Conduct

​BEA & YSpace Attendee Media & Photo Release Notice

​By attending this event, you acknowledge that the Black Entrepreneurship Alliance (BEA) and YSpace may capture photos, video, and audio for storytelling, promotional, and archival use. If you prefer not to be included in any recordings or photos, please notify a BEA or YSpace team member upon arrival so accommodations can be made.

​All guests are expected to follow event guidelines and adhere to the direction of BEA and YSpace staff to support a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment. If you experience or witness behaviour that feels inappropriate, unsafe, or out of line with our community values, please report it immediately to a BEA or YSpace team member.

​BEA reserves the right to remove or deny access to anyone who does not uphold these expectations.
