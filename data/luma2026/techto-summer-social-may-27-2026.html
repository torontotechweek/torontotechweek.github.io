About Event

​TechTO is BACK with its annual Summer Social at Bobby Bermuda's Sunset Bar (formerly The Slip)! This year, we're hosting it during Toronto Tech Week!

Our annual outdoor social event. 600 people, tacos, drinks and sunshine on Toronto’s beautiful waterfront. Need we say more?

​Get ready to connect, meet new people, and discover some exciting companies making amazing contributions to Canada's tech ecosystem!

​In past years, we’ve seen more than 600 join us to mix and mingle over drinks and bites in one of the most coveted outdoor venues in the city. This event has limited capacity and will sell out. Buy your tickets early!

​What to expect:

​We hope you'll use the opportunity to expand your network and help others with their goals.

Every ticket includes food and one beverage (alcoholic or non-alcoholic).

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
