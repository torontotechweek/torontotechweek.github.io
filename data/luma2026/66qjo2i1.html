About Event

​Cache invites Toronto's tech and fashion communities to The Closet Economy: an invite-only happy hour at the intersection of AI, influencer culture, and the future of resale.

​Join us in Yorkville for an evening of conversation, connection, and refreshments (sponsored by Nutbar). Join us in Yorkville for an evening of conversation, connection, and refreshments. Cache is an AI-powered resale platform built for influencers, turning their closets into storefronts.

​We sit at the intersection of resale, the creator economy, and community, and this is our official launch. On Thursday, May 28th from 4 to 7 PM, we're bringing together investors, founders, press, and creators in the heart of Yorkville for an elevated evening that kicks off our 4-day Toronto Tech Week activation.

​Expect curated conversations (including a fireside chat between Sarah Bartnicka of Milk Bag and Cache Founder & CEO, Devyn Olin), new connections, and a front-row seat to the future of fashion and commerce.

​—

​We’re excited to partner with Stan for a ‘Build in Public’ challenge. Attendees are encouraged to share a LinkedIn post about their experience at the event - whether that’s something they built, someone they met, or a key insight they learned throughout the evening. To enter, participants must tag both Cache and Stanley in their post. We’ll be selecting winners to receive prizes from both Cache and Nutbar!
