About Event

​DIGITAL ASSET SUMMIT CANADA🍁

​Toronto Tech Week’s Premier Digital Asset & Blockchain Event

​📍 Toronto, Canada
📅 Tuesday May 26, 2026

​Digital Asset Summit Canada is one of Web3TO’s flagship 2026 events and a premier gathering for leaders across blockchain, digital assets, AI, fintech, and emerging technology during Toronto Tech Week.

​The summit brings together government leaders, regulators, institutions, founders, investors, builders, innovators, and global industry participants to explore how Canada and the world are shaping the future of digital finance and blockchain infrastructure.

​As Canada continues to position itself as a global hub for fintech and innovation, Digital Asset Summit Canada creates a space for meaningful conversations around:

  • ​Digital asset regulation & policy

  • ​Stablecoins & digital payments

  • ​Tokenization & Real World Assets (RWA)

  • ​Institutional adoption

  • ​Custody & settlement infrastructure

  • ​Market structure & onchain liquidity

  • ​AI, blockchain & emerging technologies

  • ​The intersection of traditional finance and Web3

​The event will feature insightful panels, keynote discussions, networking opportunities, ecosystem activations, and conversations focused on the next era of innovation in Canada and globally 🌎

​Taking place during Toronto Tech Week, DAS Canada connects Canada’s thriving technology ecosystem with the rapidly evolving world of digital assets, blockchain infrastructure, AI, and institutional finance.

​⚡ Whether you’re a founder, investor, institution, developer, policymaker, or technology leader . Digital Asset Summit Canada is where the future of the industry comes together.
