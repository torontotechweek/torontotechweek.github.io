About Event

​Homecoming is the official main stage of Toronto Tech Week 2026, returning for its second year to bring together over 1,000 of Canada’s top builders from across the country and around the world.

​Hosted at History, Toronto's newest state-of-the-art live venue, Homecoming anchors a week-long, citywide celebration of Canadian technology — featuring the conversations, connections, and people shaping what comes next.

​Join over 1,000 founders, operators, and investors from the global tech community for a day of real conversations, bold ideas, and the energy of a city and country ready to build.

​FAQ

​How does approval work?

​We review requests on a rolling basis to ensure a strong mix of roles, companies, and locations in the room. Your card will only be charged if your request is approved.

​When will I hear back?

​Most requests are reviewed within a few days. You’ll receive a confirmation email if approved.

​What types of tickets are available?

​We offer a limited number of in-person tickets. Additional options may be released closer to the event.

​Are tickets refundable or transferable?

​All tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable to manage the attendee experience.

​Anything else I should know?

​All in-person tickets are subject to approval and on-site security screening.
