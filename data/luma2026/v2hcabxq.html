About Event

​​​DevTools Toronto is a community of software development tool builders, entrepreneurs, and enthusiasts.

For Demo Night, we've asked select community members to come and show us the bleeding edge tools they've been building. Everything from emerging AI tools, frameworks & libraries, to developer SaaS products.

Featuring:

  • ​Traces – Make coding agents multiplayer

  • ​Paragon – The most accurate QA agent

  • ​Superuser – Team chat with AI tools

  • ​Tidra – AI code maintenance at scale

  • ​Code Pathfinder – Trace vulnerabilities across your codebase

  • ​Docsalot – Docs your agents can read

​
Sponsored by:

  • ​OpenCode - The open source AI coding agent

  • ​Modem - your dev team's auto-triage PM

  • ​BDC - The bank for Canadian entrepreneurs

​​DevTools Toronto is taking place at BDC Square Toronto, 81 Bay St. Suite 3700. Food and non-alcoholic beverages will be provided.

NOTE: Space is limited, registration requires approval. We will prioritize applicants with an obvious link to the software industry. If you omit answers during registration that make it hard for us to figure that out, your registration may not be approved.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
