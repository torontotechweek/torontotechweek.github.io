About Event

​Most AI conversations are built for founders or engineers. This one isn't.

​As part of Toronto Tech Week, Fulfil is hosting an evening dedicated to marketers, sales, and customer success professionals who want to see how AI can fit into their day-to-day.

​Come see how you can implement AI-native workflows the next morning using tools like Claude Cowork and Claude Code.

​We’ll cover examples such as:

​- Optimizing your website and customer helpdesk docs for AI search visibility

​- Streamlining prospect and customer research to help reduce meeting prep admin

​- Improving customer product adoption through personalization

​- Automating case study creation

​- And more ways to automate your admin work

​This is also a chance to connect with our team. We're growing, and we're looking for curious, ambitious people who want to have AI at the forefront of their work.

​Come for the learning. Stay for the conversation. Limited to 50 spots. 

​Hosted by Fulfil (https://www.fulfil.io/): The AI-Native ERP for eCommerce, powering brands like Mejuri, Kotn and dbrand

​Featuring Matt Parkin, Chief of Staff at Fulfil and FoundersBeta 30 Under 30, sharing AI-native workflows with 23,000+ LinkedIn followers

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech (Torontotechweek.com).
