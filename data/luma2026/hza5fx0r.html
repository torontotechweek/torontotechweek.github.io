About Event

​CTRL+SHIFT is a proud event partner of Toronto Tech Week! Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. 

​SIGNALS VS. NOISE
For Vol. 12, we're partnering up with Pluto, a fourth space for creatives, for a session that attempts to separate signals from noise. In a world full of constant chatter and speed, we'll take a moment to discern what AI updates we should really be paying attention to and why it matters not just for our work but for our lives.

​Format:

  • ​2 talks from industry practitioners (15–20 min each)

  • ​Live art installation

  • ​The Unwind: Networking and social mixer

​CTRL+SHIFT is where Toronto's creative community comes to share work, exchange ideas, and find their signal.
