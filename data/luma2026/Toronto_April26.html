About Event

​Hey Toronto, get ready for open pitch competitions, panels and networking happening alongside Tech Week!

"Finnovation Day: Real-Time Payments, Real-Time Fraud. Can Risk Keep Up?"

​The next wave of fintech innovation is powered by real-time infrastructure and accelerated by AI. Payments are becoming instant, borders are dissolving, and new financial products are being launched faster than ever.

​But with this speed comes a new reality: fraud is evolving just as fast, and a little bit faster.

​AI is no longer just a tool for financial institutions. It’s being used on both sides:

  • ​to optimize underwriting, detection, and customer experience

  • ​and to simulate behavior, bypass controls, and scale fraud attacks

​This creates a new kind of challenge for the industry:
- How do you enable real-time innovation without opening the door to real-time fraud?
- How do you build guardrails that protect without slowing growth?

​At this Toronto Tech Week gathering, we bring together the people operating at that exact intersection. Expect builders, risk leaders, and fintech operators exploring what it really takes to keep up.

​What to expect:

​🎤 Expert Panel Discussion

​A candid conversation with representatives from Canada’s leading financial institutions and fintechs on:

  • ​the trade-offs between speed, security, and user experience

  • ​how fraud is changing in the age of AI

  • ​what actually works in modern risk infrastructure

​🚀 Startup Showcase & Competition

​Founders building the next generation of fintech innovation, including fraud prevention, risk and compliance tools will present their solutions, get feedback from industry experts and compete for an award.

​! Only FinTech and related startups are eligible to participate.

​⭐️ At the closing Prize Ceremony, one award will be presented:

  • ​​​Pitch Competition Winner

​🏆 Main Prize - Winner will receive $25,000 in AWS credit from Spendbase, our official Pitch Competition Prize Partner.
Note: The full amount is guaranteed for winners who haven't previously redeemed $25k+ in AWS credits. Others may receive an adjusted amount.

​🤝 Curated Networking

​A room designed for meaningful connections between fintech builders, risk executives, and operators shaping the future of payments.

​Join us to explore how the industry can move faster without losing control!
