About Event

​As part of Toronto Tech Week, join us at OneEleven for a special edition of our Pitch Showcase — bringing together some of the city’s most exciting startups and the people shaping Canada’s innovation ecosystem.

​This curated evening will feature 4 high-potential companies presenting to a room of 150–200 founders, investors, operators, and ecosystem leaders. Set against the backdrop of a week dedicated to celebrating Canadian tech, this event is designed to spark connection, surface opportunity, and highlight what’s next.

​OneEleven’s Pitch Showcase is more than just a demo — it’s a platform for emerging companies to gain visibility, build relationships, and accelerate momentum within Toronto’s growing tech community.

​What to expect:

  • ​Live pitches from standout OneEleven companies

  • ​A highly engaged Toronto Tech Week audience

  • ​Meaningful connections across founders, investors, and operators

  • ​A spotlight on companies building and scaling in Canada

​Whether you’re looking to discover your next investment, meet collaborators, or tap into the energy of Toronto Tech Week, this is an evening not to miss.

​6:15 PM – Startup Pitch Showcase

​6:55 PM – BDC Reverse Pitch

​7:05 PM – Special Panel (TBA)

​7:35 PM – Audience Q&A

​7:45 PM onwards – Networking & Refreshments

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
