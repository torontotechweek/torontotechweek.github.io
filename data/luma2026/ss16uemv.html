About Event

​Market Mindfulness is a beginner-friendly board game about investing, designed to make the stock market fun, social, and easy to understand.

​At the Finliti Game of Gains Fintech Tournament, players step into the role of investors and learn how emotions like fear, confidence, regret, and ambition can shape financial decisions.

​Sponsored by NEXT AI and hosted by Jennifer Schell, fintech founder of Finliti, this interactive event blends financial literacy, strategy, behavioural finance, and friendly competition. Inspired by poker, blackjack, and Dungeons & Dragons, the game takes players through bull markets, downturns, surprise events, and behavioural traps that mirror real investing scenarios.

​Whether you are a founder, builder, student, finance professional, or simply curious about the markets, this is a welcoming chance to learn, play, compete for prizes, and experience investing in a whole new way.

​About NEXT AI

​NEXT AI is a world-class accelerator for AI-enabled startups, helping founders build and scale ambitious ventures through mentorship, education, technical support, and access to an exceptional entrepreneurial network.

​About Finliti

​Finliti builds behavioural finance software for the investment industry, helping advisors and investors better understand money decisions, risk, and personality. Its Game of Gains brings that mission to life through a fun investing game, first played on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange.

​About Toronto Tech Week:

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 26–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
