About Event

​Canadian Strategic Missions Corporation and The Icebreaker Presents:

​Arctic Edge: Canadian Defence Tech & Investment

​Canada is entering a defining era for Arctic sovereignty and geopolitical strategy. As the nation strengthens its presence in the North, a new wave of homegrown defence tech startups is rising to meet critical capability needs—supported by government-driven innovation and growing interest from capital markets. Arctic Edge brings together leaders from across the innovation ecosystem, public sector, and investment community to explore the opportunities and challenges shaping Canada’s defence future.

​Agenda
8:00 – 9:00 AM — Networking & Coffee
9:00 AM – 12:00 PM — Keynotes, panel discussions, fireside chat, and curated networking breaks*
12:00 PM - 1:00 PM — Lunch & Closing Remarks

​​Join us on May 26, 2026 at this official Toronto Tech Week 2026 event for a morning of insight, connection, and forward-looking discussion at the intersection of defence, technology, and investment.
