About Event

​We care about the future of work.

​Join us for Open Studio, a space to explore your creativity, connect with others, and simply create without judgment. Whether you want to doodle, colour, write, flip through a magazine or just relax, this is your time to do what inspires you, alongside like-minded folks.

​Open Studio is more than a creative outlet. It's a place to find rest, connection, and a little relief from daily pressures, a communal experience rooted in care and play.

​If you're in town for Toronto Tech Week, this is a great opportunity to go deeper with the people you've been meeting, away from the noise, in a space that actually lets you connect.

​​​✨ A portion of proceeds from each will be donated to the Parkdale Food Bank, supporting nourishment and community care in our neighbourhood.

​​​Let’s create together.

​​​About Re-Work

​Re-Work is a Community First

​We're a professional (and personal) development community helping people redefine work, ambition, and success on their own terms. We want you to get to a healthier, less stress-filled place with the thing we spend most of our lives doing: working. Move beyond burnout toward work that is meaningful and fulfilling as your fullest self. Through a proven process and framework, we help you craft a more sustainable and values-aligned relationship with work. Through workshops, storytelling, and community spaces, Re-Work invites you to explore a healthier, more sustainable way of working and living.

​
Let’s build a future of work that works for you.

​About Toronto Tech Week

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
