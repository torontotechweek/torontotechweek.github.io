About Event

​Calling out all real-time ops enthusiasts in the Toronto area! Join this PagerDuty User Group and connect with PagerDuty users, share your experiences, and learn new ways to maximize the power of digital operations. It's a space where technical leaders and practitioners come together to collaborate, solve challenges, and get inspired by each other's successes.

​What can you expect of a PagerDuty User Group?
User groups focus solely on learning and growth—no sales pitches, just real conversations about what works (and sometimes, what doesn’t!). You’ll have the opportunity to dive deep into topics like incident management, automation, and DevOps best practices, while building meaningful connections with peers.

​Who Can Join?
System administrators, DevOps professionals, IT managers, and anyone involved in incident management and response. It’s a place to share ideas, the good, the bad, and the ugly (though we’re aiming for mostly good!).
