About Event

​ AAAI-Nigeria and PopIn Present:

​AI Without Borders: Africa–Canada Tech Collaboration & Innovation Forum

​AI Without Borders is a dynamic international forum designed to connect technology leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, policymakers, and researchers from Africa and Canada. The event explores the transformative potential of artificial intelligence, software innovation, and digital technologies across key sectors including business, trade, healthcare, education, and sustainable development.

​Through expert-led panel discussions, interactive sessions, and strategic networking opportunities, participants will examine practical applications of AI, opportunities for cross-border technology collaboration, and pathways for scaling innovation globally. The forum will also highlight emerging opportunities for startup development, technology commercialization, and international knowledge exchange between African and Canadian ecosystems.

​The event welcomes founders, engineers, investors, creators, students, policymakers, and business professionals interested in leveraging artificial intelligence and emerging technologies to drive innovation and economic growth.

​By bridging two continents, AI Without Borders aims to foster meaningful partnerships, promote knowledge sharing, and develop actionable strategies that accelerate technology adoption and responsible AI development in global markets.
