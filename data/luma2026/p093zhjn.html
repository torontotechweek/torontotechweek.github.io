About Event

​Creative Room is a curated evening for those shaping digital experiences across Toronto — designers, creatives, and brand-minded builders.

​

​Hosted by Designers & Coffee during Toronto Tech Week, this gathering brings together people who care deeply about craft — from product and UX to branding, storytelling, and digital experience.

​

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 26–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech.

​

​The evening unfolds into a social atmosphere where conversations flow naturally — a space for perspective, taste, and connection beyond panels and typical networking.

​

​Expect good music, a curated raffle giveaway, and a few special moments and announcements throughout the evening. Drinks will be available for purchase at the bar.

​

​If you’ve RSVP’d, we’re looking forward to having you. As plans evolve, please keep your RSVP up to date so we can shape the room accordingly.
