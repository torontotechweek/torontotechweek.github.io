About Event

​Join us for a special Toronto Tech Week × Ecom Network evening bringing together founders, operators, and those interested in the intersection of tech and commerce. This high-energy networking social is designed for people building, scaling, or enabling modern ecommerce businesses: from software and platforms to brands and marketplaces.✨

​📸Follow us on Instagram @Ecomnetworking📸

​Connect with ecommerce founders, SaaS builders, product leaders, growth operators, and tech entrepreneurs over great drinks and real conversations. Whether you’re building a product, scaling a brand, or powering commerce behind the scenes, this is a chance to meet others doing genuinely interesting work across Toronto’s tech and ecommerce ecosystem. 🥂💬

​Who is this event for?

​This event is ideal for:

  • ​DTC & ecommerce founders (Shopify, Amazon FBA, marketplaces)

  • ​SaaS, AI, fintech, martech, and infrastructure builders serving commerce

  • ​Operators in growth, product, data, engineering, and operations

  • ​Anyone exploring the future of ecommerce, technology, and digital business

​Whether you’re running a brand, building tools for sellers, or working on the tech that powers online commerce - you’ll be in the right room.

​Event Highlights

​✨ Connect Across Tech & Commerce
Meet people building both products and brands, from founders and engineers to operators and growth leaders, in a relaxed, social setting.

​📣 Share What You’re Building
Working on a startup, product, platform, or brand? Looking for feedback, partnerships, or collaborators? This is the perfect environment to introduce yourself and spark meaningful conversations.

​💡 Learn From the Ecosystem
Swap insights on scaling, product, growth, AI, marketing, operations, and sales. Past attendees have formed partnerships, landed clients, and uncovered new opportunities - you never know where one great conversation might lead.

​Dress code: Smart/business casual

​About Us:

​Ecom Network is one of the fastest-growing communities of ecommerce founders, brand builders, and online sellers. Through curated monthly events around the world, we bring together entrepreneurs and operators to connect, share insights, and scale their businesses.

About Toronto Tech Week

Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
