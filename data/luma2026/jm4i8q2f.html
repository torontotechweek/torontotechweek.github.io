About Event

​The AI in Retail (AiR) Meetup is a curated gathering of retail leaders, AI innovators, founders, and investors exploring how artificial intelligence is reshaping the future of commerce.

​Presented by RETHINK Retail in partnership with the University of Toronto’s Schwartz Reisman Innovation Campus, the event takes place during Toronto Tech Week and connects the global retail industry with one of North America’s fastest growing technology ecosystems.

​

​

​The program brings together leaders from retail, AI, venture, and enterprise technology for an evening of strategic content and high-intent networking designed to accelerate collaboration across the commerce and technology sectors.

​The AiR Conference & Reception is part of a series of events presented by RETHINK Retail, a global community advancing practical AI adoption across the retail industry. The largest of its kind AI in Retail (AiR) Conference & Reception has been held annually for 5 years now in New York City during the Retail Big Show Event via a direct partnership with the National Retail Federation.

​IN PARTNERSHIP WITH:

​ZeroStone AI

​ZeroStone is a Toronto-based AI transformation firm that helps mid-market organizations find where AI creates real value, then builds production-grade systems and hands off the capability to run them independently. Founded by operators and engineers who have built and scaled companies, ZeroStone combines technical depth with a human-centered approach to make transformation last. ZeroStone works with Canadian retailers to transform how they create demand and convert customers, with custom AI built on their brand, data and workflows.
