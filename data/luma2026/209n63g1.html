About Event

​The best way to learn about the Toronto startup and tech scene is to talk to people in it right now.

​We're hosting a panel during Toronto Tech Week bringing together some impressive young builders and operators in the city, all of whom have built real personal brands and audiences alongside what they're doing.

​We'll get into what it actually looks like to get started in the scene, how to leverage your personal brand, what they're working on now, the tradeoffs they're making, and the advice they wish they'd heard earlier.

​Confirmed panelists:

  • ​Jack Price (Host)

  • ​Anson Lin

  • ​Sammy Tourani

  • ​Daniel Park

  • ​Nabeeha Ahmed

​This panel is for students wondering which direction to go, or any young person who wants to be involved at the frontier of innovation.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
