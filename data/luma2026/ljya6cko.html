About Event

​​Ship What Matters: R&D at Wealthsimple

​Join us on the Wealthsimple HQ rooftop for one of the best nights of Toronto Tech Week - a candid panel followed by an open rooftop social with the people building Canada's most-loved financial products.

You'll hear from our engineering & product leaders on how we build and ship together - the real story behind the decisions, trade-offs, and products that make a difference in the lives of millions of Canadians. Then stick around for drinks, bites, and great conversations, and get a behind-the-scenes look at our tech, culture, and career opportunities.

This is an evening for engineers, product managers, and R&D builders who care about shipping meaningful work - and who want to meet the people doing it at Wealthsimple. Plus, we're giving away $500 in gold on the night! 🥇

​Who you'll meet:

  • ​Diederik van Liere, Chief Technology Officer

  • ​Polly D'Arcy, VP Design

  • ​​John Grant, VP Engineering

  • ​Claudia Labal, Product Manager

  • ​& other Wealthsimplers

​⚡ Space in our office is limited - register to request your spot and you'll receive a confirmation once capacity has been reviewed.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
