About Event

​About the Event

​THE SOVEREIGN GALA: GENESIS is not your typical Toronto networking event. This will be the most prestigious event of Toronto Tech Week! Prepare for an evening of elegance, mystery, and powerful connections with entrepreneurs, investors, founders, and influential leaders.

​We are Proud to be an Official Partner of Toronto Tech Week.

​Set in a luxurious black-tie atmosphere, this masquerade blends elegance with connection. A selection of Gourmet food, and an Open Bar to share conversations. Behind the masks, guests you will meet include some of the most ambitious minds across Tech, AI, Real Estate, Venture Capital, and Startups. Expect a refined social environment where meaningful conversations, partnerships, and new ideas can emerge naturally.

​What To Expect

​🍽️ Food Experience

​Enjoy elevated food stations throughout the night:

​• Oven-Baked Custom Pizza Station – Fresh pizzas made to order
• Chef-Curated Gourmet Bites served throughout the evening
• Artisan Gelato Station with premium flavors
• Delicious dinner plates perfect for mingling and socializing

​🍸 Drinks & Bar

​A premium bar experience designed for the night:

​• Signature Masquerade Cocktails crafted exclusively for the event
• Open Bar with premium spirits, wine, and beer
• Drinks curated by professional mixologists

​🎭 Black & Gold Masquerade

​Enter a stunning black-and-gold themed venue where masks and black-tie attire create an atmosphere of elegance and intrigue.
The masquerade setting adds mystery while making conversations more relaxed and memorable.

​(If you dont have a Masquerade Mask, they will be provided)

​💎 GEMSTONE FINANCE PRESENTS

​💍 An Exclusive Gemstone Auction

​Browse and bid in a silent gemstone auction. featuring a limited collection of unique certified precious gems from the Gemstone Finance vaults. Timeless beauty and value presented in a curated gemstone browsing experience, and at the end of the night, they can be yours forever. We will be announcing more specifics as we draw closer to the Gala.

​🎟️ WIN a Precious Gemstone

​Every attendee will also be entered into a draw to win a Certified Precious Gemstone with an appraised value of over $1000! Details to be announced.

​🥂 Luxury Social Atmosphere

​Expect a vibrant environment with great music by DJ Talmor, great people, and meaningful conversations, all designed to spark new ideas, partnerships, and opportunities.

​🚐 Luxury Limo Shuttle/Party Bus

​For a seamless experience, complimentary limo shuttles will run between downtown Toronto and the venue throughout the night. (7 Min Drive) (Details will be shared with approved guests)

​Dress Code:

​An evening of elegance and mystery. Guests are expected to wear formal black tie attire paired with a masquerade mask.

​Gentlemen are expected to wear a tuxedo or any color formal suit with bow tie.

​Ladies are encouraged to wear evening gowns or elegant cocktail dresses.

​Masquerade masks are highly encouraged to embrace the mystery and atmosphere of the evening. (masks will be provided if you dont have your own)

​Colors that complement the event aesthetic:
Black, white, gold, metallics, and deep jewel/gemstone tones.

​🔞 Age Requirement: 19+
