About Event

​Land & Expand celebrates arrival and ambition: the decision to choose the Toronto Region, and what becomes possible once that choice is made. In the GTA, expansion can mean many things, from new partnerships and wider networks to bigger teams, broader operations, and greater impact across the region. The evening brings together the people and organizations who help make both parts of that story possible.

​Set against the backdrop of one of the world’s most dynamic city-regions, attendees will have the opportunity to connect with investors, industry executives, government partners, and ecosystem builders who are shaping the GTA’s economic future.

Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
