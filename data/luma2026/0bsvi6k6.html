About Event

​What does it take to rebuild a go-to-market system from the ground up at DoorDash scale?

Join us during Toronto Tech Week for an inside look at how DoorDash’s GTM Engineering team is rethinking the foundation of sales, growth, and merchant experiences. We’re tackling a deeply complex problem space shaped by fragmented tools, acquisitions, and manual workflows—and transforming it into a unified, AI-native platform.

In this session, you’ll hear directly from engineering leaders and builders working on systems that power go-to-market across every segment we serve. We’ll go beyond high-level vision and dive into real technical challenges, live demos, and early explorations in AI-driven workflows—from intelligent merchant discovery to automating pre-sales processes.

This isn’t just about what we’ve built, it’s about what comes next. We’ll share how we’re embedding AI into core systems, standardizing experiences across segments, and moving toward a fully unified, AI-first GTM stack.

If you’re excited by solving ambiguous, high-impact problems, building 0→1 systems, and turning AI experimentation into production-grade products, we’d love to meet you.

Stick around after the panel for food, drinks, and the chance to network with our engineers and hiring managers!
