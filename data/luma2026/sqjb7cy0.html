​About Event

​

​Beyond the Baseline: Women’s Health x AI

​A curated evening spotlighting women founders building at the intersection of health and AI as part of the Toronto Tech Week.

​This session brings together founders working across biomedical AI, FemTech, and data-driven care to share how they are building solutions in Fertility, PCOS, and Menopause.

​Featuring:
Suze & Mindy — Sprout Family
Julie — July Health

​More speakers to come!

​If you’ve ever thought,
“Why does it feel like women’s health is an afterthought in innovation? What is being done about this?”

​
This room is for you.

​What You’ll Experience

​✨ A high-impact founder panel at the frontier of health + AI
✨ Honest conversations on building in under-researched spaces
✨ Interactive breakout activity
✨ Live audience Q&A
✨ Curated goodies + giveaway
✨ A room full of builders, thinkers, and future shapers of care

​📘 What We’ll Explore

​AI in Women’s Health
How founders are applying AI to real-world challenges across fertility, PCOS, menopause, and beyond.

​Building Where Data is Missing
What it actually takes to create in spaces that have been historically ignored.

​The Next Wave of Innovation
Where capital, talent, and technology are moving, and what’s still being overlooked.

​From Conversation to Creation
Turning insights into action through interactive group discussions.

​📖 Agenda

​6:30 – 7:00 PM — Guest Arrival + Mingle
Settle in, grab a drink, and connect with others in the room.

​7:00 – 7:10 PM — Opening Remarks

​7:10 – 8:00 PM — Founder Panel
A moderated conversation with Suze, Mindy, Julie, and other powerful builders on building at the edge of women's health + AI.

​8:00 – 8:10 PM — Audience Q&A

​8:10 – 8:30 PM — Interactive Case Study Session
Small-group case study where you get to:

  • ​Address one real gap our founders are working to solve in women’s health today

  • ​Showcase, would you address this problem if constraints didn’t exist?

  • ​Potentially win a prize as a team!

​8:30 – 8:40 PM — Group Sharebacks + Closing Reflections
Key insights, final thoughts, and takeaways.

​8:40 – 9:30 PM — Networking + Giveaway
Stay, connect, and continue the conversation.

​✋ Format

​In-person. Interactive. Thought-provoking.
