About Event

​Toronto Tech Week 2026
OpenClaw x Kuvi.ai

​You didn't hear this from us. But something's happening on May 27th. And you're invited.

​This is the after-hours event Tech Week didn't plan for. Part demo night, part proper party. The kind of evening where you leave with three new contacts, one idea you can't stop thinking about, and a slightly sore neck from dancing.

​We've got a guest DJ. Food and bar. And we've got people in the room who are actually building the next thing.

​What to expect:

​🍽️ Mingle + Food — Doors open, drinks are flowing, introductions are happening. Get comfortable. The crowd is the feature.

​🎤 Keynote: Kuvi.ai — How do you beat Wall Street at its own game? Kuvi is building the programmable strategy layer for finance — letting anyone translate intent into automated strategies that execute across markets in real time. Institutional-grade coordination, for everyone. Big vision. Real product. Right now.

​⚡ Two Live Demos — Short, sharp, and kind of unbelievable. OpenClaw community members showing what an autonomous agent actually does when you let it loose in the real world. Expect the unexpected.

​🕺 Dance Party until Midnight — DJ takes over. Bar stays open. You earned it.

​Come for the future of finance. Stay for everything else.
