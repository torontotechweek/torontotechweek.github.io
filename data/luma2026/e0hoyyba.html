About Event

​You're invited to Startup Sweats, a curated fitness series for the Toronto tech community.

​Sponsored by Alan

​Startup Sweats is back for Toronto Tech Week 2026, bigger and bolder than last year. Join 100+ founders, builders, operators, and friends of the Toronto tech community for a 50 minute group movement session and bootcamp-style workout. After the sweat, stick around for healthy drinks, snacks, and the chance to connect with great people from across the community.

​Spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-serve basis. You won't want to miss this workout!

​​​​​Details

​About the Coach

​​​Zalie Tshuma specializes in leading large group movement sessions, bringing energy and expertise to classes, workshops, events and corporate activations. She creates engaging, music-driven fitness experiences that inspire participation, energize crowds, and leave a lasting impression. Zalie is the founder of Burn Room, a boutique Toronto fitness studio, and a winner of the ClassPass Best Instructor award.

​About Toronto Tech Week

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
