About Event

​​Join us for the Notion Toronto Community Conference Day during Toronto Tech Week!

​Join Toronto's community of Notion users for a day of talks, panels, workshops, interactive activities and more!

​​This event is an official part of Toronto Tech Week :)

​Expect:

  • ​Interesting talks, panel discussions and keynotes (line-up coming soon)

  • ​Workshops and interactive sessions

  • ​​Fun breakout activities and interactive Notion games

  • ​​Live jazz trio!!!

  • ​​Notion Toronto Community's now-renowned snack selection

  • ​Personalized 'Notion Faces' name badges for all attendees

  • ​​Some sweet Notion swag

  • ​A gorgeous, open and airy venue space

  • ​​A campy Notion vibe

  • ​​And, most importantly, tons of interesting Notion people to mingle, meet and rub elbows with :)

​Venue Partner: BrainStation

​This event is being hosted at the wonderful BrainStation, located downtown near Front St. & Spadina Ave., with easy TTC access and ample parking options.

​BrainStation is a global leader in digital skills training and workforce transformation. Founded in 2012, BrainStation has certified over 35,000 professionals and worked with some of the largest companies in the world through cutting-edge digital skills training delivered online and in person from campuses in New York, London, and Toronto.

​Toronto Tech Week:

​​This event is an official part of Toronto Tech Week 2026 - a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech | torontotechweek.com

​More details:

​​​There's no cost to attend this event, but please make sure that if you do book a spot here through the event page, you end up coming! Space will be limited, and we want to make sure that everyone interested in attending will be able to.

​​All attendees must be registered, and will be scanned in at the door using the Luma check-in QR codes provided in your registration confirmations.
