About Event

​Join Deel & Sterling for Brunch with Benefits & Payroll during Toronto Tech Week. A curated morning event where Canada's startup community comes together to talk about one of the most underrated levers in building a great company: taking care of your people.

Over a relaxed breakfast, we'll hear from a guest speaker on why employee benefits and payroll aren't just back-office logistics for tech startups; they're foundational for attracting and retaining the people who actually move the needle.

​Then, we'll officially launch Series Human, a new collaborative program from Deel and Sterling built specifically for Canadian startups. Because when it comes to growing your company, your team is the most important round you'll ever raise.

Come for coffee. Stay for the conversation.

Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
