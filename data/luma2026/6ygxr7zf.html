About Event

​Overview
An exclusive, invite-only recovery experience for VCs, accelerator operators, and ecosystem connectors - hosted the Sunday before Toronto Tech Week.

​Relax and unwind in a premium performance wellness space designed to reset your body and mind.

​Hosted at RCVRI Toronto, in collaboration with Founder Athlete Inc., this immersive session combines recovery, and an intentional reset.

​The Experience

​You’ll move through a curated experience that includes:

  • ​Hot & Cold Plunge Pools — reset your nervous system and boost clarity

  • ​Red Light Therapy – support recovery, boost energy, and enhance focus

  • ​​PEMF Therapy – optimize circulation and cellular performance

  • ​Compression Therapy – relieve tension and accelerate recovery

  • ​Vitamin B12 shot - administered by an RPN

​RCVRI's team will guide you through each modality so you get the most out of the experience.

​Why This Matters

​Get your body and mind prepped for the marathon that is Tech Week.

By Monday morning you'll be flooded with LinkedIn messages, cold emails, and a long-list of busy events.

The Pre-Game helps you get set so you're locked in and ready to perform at your best.

​Come to reset. Stay to connect.

​About Founder Athlete Inc.

​
Founder Athlete Inc. builds performance infrastructure for high-growth and scaling founders.

​Building a venture-backed company is a high-performance sport, but founders don't have the capacity for high execution output without burning out.

​At Founder Athlete, we engineer high-performing founders capable of enduring the demands of the founder journey over the long-run.

​Using the Foundlete Method™, we integrate three core pillars directly into a founder’s daily operating system:

  • ​Physical State — optimizing the biological foundation that drives energy, focus, and recovery

  • ​Mental Psychology — building resilience to execute under sustained pressure

  • ​Execution Systems — installing structured frameworks for deep work, prioritization, and decision-making

​The result: founders who don’t just push harder, but are fit to build and built to win the founder journey.

​About the Venue

​
RCVRI Toronto is the home of performance wellness, located at 8 Mercer Street. Built for high performers, it offers cutting-edge recovery modalities designed to optimize both physical and mental performance.

​Toronto Tech Week

​
Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
