About Event

​What will crypto development look like in 12, 24, and 36 months? Uniblock’s CEO Kevin Callahan hosts a panel of leading crypto operators, founders, and executives to unpack their predictions and hot takes - what’s breaking, what’s emerging, and what technical leaders should be planning for now. This session is designed for crypto founders, CTOs, product and engineering teams looking to hear unfiltered perspectives.

​

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
