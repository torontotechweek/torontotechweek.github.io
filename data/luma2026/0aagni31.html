About Event

​How Autonomous AI Will Transform Travel in 2026 and Beyond

​What happens when AI agents start planning, booking, and managing travel on our behalf?

​A new generation of agentic AI systems is emerging, capable of making decisions, executing complex tasks, and managing entire travel workflows. From trip planning and booking to real-time itinerary management, these autonomous systems could fundamentally reshape how travel is discovered, purchased, and experienced.

​During Toronto Tech Week, join Travel Trends Podcast and Intrepid Travel for a special live podcast recording exploring one of the most important technological shifts facing the travel industry.

​This interactive evening will feature an incredible panel of travel industry executives, startup entrepreneurs, and technology leaders discussing how agentic AI is already beginning to impact travel companies and what it means for the future of the industry in 2026 and beyond.

​Unlike a traditional panel, this will be a live podcast experience, with opportunities for audience questions, interaction, and discussion during the recording.

​What to Expect

  • ​Live recording of the Travel Trends Podcast

  • ​Expert panel discussion on Agentic AI and the future of travel

  • ​Audience interaction and Q&A

  • ​Startup demos and partner booths showcasing Toronto’s travel tech ecosystem

  • ​Networking party with founders, operators, investors, and travel innovators

  • ​Fun CPG swag by companies supporting your next travel experience (No Days Wasted, Zenergy, Greenhouse Juice Co, and more!)

​After the recording, stay for a fantastic networking reception designed to help connect the people shaping the future of travel. Meet fellow builders, exchange ideas, and deepen relationships across the travel and technology ecosystem.

​Whether you're a founder, travel executive, technologist, investor, or operator, this evening is an opportunity to learn, connect, and help shape how AI will transform our industry.

​Come curious. Leave connected.

​🌍 Speakers

​🎤 Dan Christian
Founder & Host, Travel Trends Podcast

​Dan Christian is a global travel industry executive, entrepreneur, and founder/host of the Travel Trends Podcast—the world’s #1 B2B travel podcast with listeners in over 125 countries. A seasoned leader, he previously served as Chief Digital Officer at The Travel Corporation, overseeing global digital transformation and marketing across 40 brands. Dan has also held senior roles at Lonely Planet and G Adventures bringing deep expertise across travel, media, and loyalty.

​🎤 Christian Wolters
President for Canada and GM of Marketing, Intrepid Travel

​Christian Wolters is a travel executive with 20+ years growing brands like Intrepid Travel and TourRadar. Intrepid is the world’s largest adventure travel company and a B Corp, offering sustainable small group tours in 120+ countries. Christian drives growth in North America, focusing on revenue, partnerships, and innovation, and is passionate about using technology to enhance efficiency and personalization while preserving the human touch.

​🎤 Brian Tossan
Chief Technology Officer, Toronto Pearson International Airport

​Brian Tossan brings more than two decades of extensive experience to his role as the leader of Information Technology and Innovation at Toronto Pearson International Airport, serving as a driving force for technological advancement, pioneering new horizons in airport operations and enhancing passenger experiences through human-centric solutions.

​🎤 Jeff Kischuk
Co-Founder & CEO, Tripian

​Jeff Kischuk is Co-founder and CEO of Tripian, a B2B travel infrastructure company. He built the verified, structured data layer and orchestration engine that powers agentic AI across card networks, cruise operators, tourism boards, and global DMCs. An engineer and serial founder (four times over), Jeff has been running Tripian's Nested AI® platform, purpose-built for agentic execution, in production since 2016.
