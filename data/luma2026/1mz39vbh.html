About Event

​Are you looking to break into, transition within, or thrive in Product Management in the age of AI? This event is for you.

Hosted by Iris Guo, a former AI PM at Zynga, Scale AI, Microsoft, and Google X, and a Career Coach for 4+ years, and Aleeza Ladhani, a PM at Sago Mini, a Harvard M.Ed., and a former chocolate company founder — generously sponsored by Sago Mini — this event blends learning with meaningful connection.

We’ll begin with a speaker panel exploring how AI is reshaping the PM landscape — from navigating career transitions into product to helping mid–senior PMs evolve their skillsets and stay competitive in an AI-first world.

Following the panel, we’ll move into a job search clinic hosted by Iris to offer practical guidance on positioning yourself for your next move — whether you're breaking in, pivoting, or levelling up.

The evening concludes with a happy hour networking session, where you can connect with peers across experience levels — from aspiring PMs to seasoned operators — in a relaxed, community-driven environment.

🗓 Agenda

5:00 – 5:30 PM | Check-in & Enjoy Refreshments
5:30 – 6:30 PM | Speaker Panel
6:30 – 7:30 PM | Job Search Clinic
6:30 – 8:30 PM | Networking & Happy Hour

Whether you're making a career transition, actively job searching, or looking to future-proof your PM skillset, this event is designed to give you the insights and connections to move forward with confidence in the AI era.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
