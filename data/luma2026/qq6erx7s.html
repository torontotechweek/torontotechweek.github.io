About Event

​Grab a coffee. Take a walk. Talk shop with the people actually building the future of education.

​CoLab Coffee Club is a casual Toronto Tech Week meetup for EdTech founders, educators, and ecosystem partners who care about nurturing relationships, sharing insights, and building a better future for learners (young & old). No panels. No lanyards. No pitch.

​We'll meet at Waterworks Food Hall (Queen West) for coffee and a bite, then head out at 8:30 for a 45-minute walk through Trinity Bellwoods area. Bring a problem of practice, your half-baked idea, or your latest not-so-hot take on AI in classrooms.

​Who should come:

  • ​Founders building in EdTech, learning, or workforce

  • ​Educators, school leaders, and PD providers shaping what learning looks like in practice

  • ​Ecosystem partners — funders, accelerators, associations, researchers, and anyone moving the space forward

​About CoLab

​CoLab is collaboration infrastructure for K-12. We help schools, districts, PD providers, and associations build the kind of communities of practice that actually change what happens in classrooms. Plus a free, open community where educators swap resources, notes, and reflections to make education better, together.
