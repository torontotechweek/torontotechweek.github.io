​About the event

​AI tools have changed how work gets done — but most people are still using Claude like a smarter search bar. What does it look like to actually get leverage from it?

​This interactive session brings together professionals, builders, and the AI-curious to learn how to use Claude effectively in real work. This is not a product pitch or a passive demo — it is a hands-on, task-driven hour focused on automating something you actually do.

​Show and Share

​The session opens with a live walkthrough of the different ways to use Claude — conversational chat, projects, and Claude Code — and where each one earns its keep in real workflows.

​Learn by Doing

​Participants will then work on their own task, with guidance, to explore:

  • ​How to break a real task into a prompt Claude can act on

  • ​When to use chat vs. projects vs. agentic modes

  • ​Prompting habits that separate "neat trick" from "saves me three hours a week"

  • ​Where Claude falls short and how to design around its limits

​Each participant brings one task from their real work — a recurring email, a messy spreadsheet, a research summary, a meeting prep ritual, anything you'd rather not do again — and automates it live with us in the room.

​The session will conclude with a brief recap of patterns that worked across the group, followed by open networking for those who want to continue the conversation.

​What to bring: A laptop and one task you do every week that you wish you didn't.
