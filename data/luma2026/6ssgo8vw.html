About Event

​Land to Innovate Presents "Reimagining Education in the Age of AI" Part of Toronto Tech Week Event.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.

​Join us on May 25th 2026 to see how artificial intelligence is reshaping how we teach, learn, and prepare the next generation.

​Reimagining Education in the Age of AI is a youth-driven innovation event hosted by Land to Innovate at Webtree Academy, bringing together educators, technology leaders, startups, and students to explore how AI is transforming the future of learning. Unlike traditional conferences, this event places young innovators at the center, giving youth entrepreneurs the opportunity to pitch ideas, engage with EdTech companies, and drive meaningful discussions about the future of education.

​For Detailed Event Agenda Visit www.landtoinnovate.org

​Time Session Details

​10:00 – 11:00 Youth Startup Pitch Youth teams pitch AI + education ideas. Judged by industry & academia.

​11:00 – 12:00 Youth Inspired Talks Short talks from youth leaders and founders on AI, startups, and education.

​12:00 – 1:00 Lunch & Showcase Networking lunch + demo booths from EdTech, AI platforms & student projects.

​1:00 – 2:00 Breakout Roundtables 4 rooms: AI Classroom | EdTech Startups | Future Skills | AI Tutors.

​2:00 – 3:00 Panel Discussion "What Should Education Look Like in 2035?"

​3:00 – 4:00 Open Networking Connect with speakers, founders, educators, and investors.

​Join us in reimagining education for the AI generation.
