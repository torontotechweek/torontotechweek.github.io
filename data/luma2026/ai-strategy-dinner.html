About Event

​Join us during Toronto Tech Week for our intimate, closed-door dinner roundtable discussion focused on emerging AI trends and practical strategy considerations for 2026. This private discussion is designed for senior leaders and decision-makers responsible for technology, data, and business strategy.

​The roundtable will be held in a private dining space at an exclusive members-only wine club with a maximum capacity of 12 guests, to ensure a candid, high-signal conversation among peers. Attendance is subject to approval to maintain a focused, leadership-only audience and "Chatham House Rules" apply.

​This roundtable is intended for thoughtful, off-the-record discussion (Chatham House rule) around where AI strategy is heading, what is proving durable in enterprise settings, and how leaders should prepare their organizations for the next phase of adoption.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
