About Event

​We’re opening up the Stan Toronto office for a special Toronto Tech Week co-working session specifically for founders and builders. Drop in anytime between 1–6pm to get focused work done outside the buzz of Tech Week alongside other ambitious operators, meet the Stan and Venn teams and get optional live feedback on your startup or idea from Ahmed Shafik (Co-Founder of Venn) and Vitalii Dodonov (Co-Founder & CTO of Stan).

​This is a no stress, high-signal space to co-work, jam on ideas, and pressure-test what you’re building. If you want, you can opt into short, informal pitch feedback sessions with the Stan/Venn team and other guest operators throughout the afternoon.

​Who it’s for

  • ​Founders and early-stage startup teams

  • ​Builders working on products, side projects, or new ideas

  • ​Engineers, designers, and operators in the Toronto tech ecosystem

​What to expect

  • ​Drop-in co-working (come and go anytime between 1–6pm)

  • ​Optional pitch feedback from experienced founders/operators

  • ​High-quality peer connections with serious builders

  • ​Food and drinks provided by Venn

​Good to know

  • ​Space is limited, all applications will be reviewed individually

  • ​Bring your laptop

  • ​Hosted the same day as Toronto Tech Week Homecoming (morning event). Come straight from the event to get a few hours of work/networking done.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
