About Event

​✨ Baddies & Bots: Women’s Fintech & AI Meetup ✨

​Calling all Toronto girlies!

​Come join us this Tech Week for a no-pressure AI & Fintech networking night for women in Toronto. If you're already in tech, or just getting started, this is the place to expand your mind and meet women in the field.

​This is for the girls who want to understand what’s happening, get in the room early, and connect with other women who are thinking bigger.

​No pressure. No gatekeeping. Just real conversations, smart women, and a space to explore what’s next.

​Come learn, connect, and see where you fit into the future of it all. 💻✨

​💬 What to Expect:

  • ​Chill, no-pressure networking (no awkward vibes)

  • ​Guided networking prompts to help facilitate real conversations

  • ​Women across AI, fintech, business & curiosity stages

  • ​Real conversations about money, tech, and opportunities

  • ​A space to ask questions, share ideas, and expand your thinking with other women

​🪩 Hosted by Growth For Girlies, Partnered with Toronto Tech Week:

Founded by Alicia Tghlian — Mindset Coach, Behavior Scientist, and the heart behind @growthforgirlies with over 100,000 Followers across social media platforms — this event brings together behavior science, subconscious rewiring, and feminine magic to help you finally feel like you again.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
