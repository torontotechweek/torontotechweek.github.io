About Event

​The Vision

​We’re bringing the OpenClaw and GOAT Network stack to Toronto for a one-day vibe coding sprint with the support of the MindFuel grant program, GOAT Network, ClawUP and Metis Foundation. Your mission: Build an AI agent that doesn’t just talk but owns its identity, manages its own wallet and transact with other agents. Leverage Bitcoin’s 99.9% uptime and BitVM2’s mathematical security (viat GOAT mainnet) to build agents that handle high-value transactions with zero human oversight.

​🏆 The Stakes

​We aren't giving out stickers and socks. We are giving away Mac Mini to the top-performing team or individual to power your future AI development. We also are looking for the bright minds to join our team and our partner projects as an interns to continue developing what they started at the hackathon. We also will be mentoring and helping those who would like to develop their hackathon projects further as their own startups.

​If you are a company and would like to partner with the event by hiring the students as an interns or by providing a prize or mentorship please send your inquiries to [email protected].

​🚀 The Challenge

​Deploy a "vibe-coded" autonomous agent using:

  • ​OpenClaw: The brain and framework.

  • ​ERC-8004: On-chain identity (Give your agent a soul).

  • ​x402: Agent-native payments (Let your agent get paid).

  • ​GOAT Network: The infrastructure for the agent economy.

​📅 Event Details

  • ​When: May 26 2026 during the Toronto Tech Week

  • ​Where: Toronto Metropolitan University (Sheldon & Tracy Levy Student Learning Centre).

  • ​Who: University students

  • ​Perks: Fuel provided (Pizza, snacks, and drinks) + On-site technical mentorship.
