About Event

​Who are we?

​We are the AI platform for post-acute care operations.

​Exacare ai brings referrals, admissions analysis, and reimbursement workflows into one place so care teams can move faster, make smarter decisions, and stay focused on what matters most. Today, over 1,500 post-acute care teams trust Exacare to run their operations.

​We’re one of the fastest-growing startups in the space, and we’re just getting started.

​Want to learn more? Check out our website and follow us on LinkedIn!

​We're built by people from world-class teams.

​Our team comes from some of the world’s most high-performing organizations, people who’ve built and scaled systems at companies like McKinsey, Bain, BCG, Amazon, Google, Apple, Airbnb, NVIDIA, and more.

​But what brings us together isn’t just experience, it’s intention. Many of us were looking for a deeper sense of impact: a chance to apply our skills to something meaningful, where the results of our work are tangible and immediate. At ExaCare, that’s exactly what we’re building toward.

​About the event.

​Where AI meets healthcare, and you meet us!

Join us for an evening of great ideas, good people, and real opportunity. We’ll start with a forward-looking talk from our CEO, then move into a lively happy hour with a live DJ setting the vibe.

Throughout the evening, you’ll find interactive moments designed to get you involved, not just watching. Meet the team, explore what we’re building, and see how we’re applying AI in post-acute care. You’ll also get a real sense of our values, our culture, and the problems we care about solving.

Come for the conversation, stay for the energy, and leave with a clear sense of what we’re building and who we’re looking to build it with.
