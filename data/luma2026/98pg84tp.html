About Event

​Focused market intelligence on Mexico and direct connections with trade and innovation leaders actively operating in the market.

​Attend this event in person in downtown Toronto and gain timely market intelligence on Mexico, one of Canada’s most important and fast-growing trade and innovation partners. Connect with leaders in trade, technology, and cross-border growth actively working between Canada and Mexico.

​Hosted at lunchtime, the session is designed for high-value conversations among founders, operators, and trade experts. This event is part of our Emerging Markets in the Spotlight series, featuring seven countries and regions throughout the year to help Canadian SMBs identify and access new global markets.

​Agenda

​12:00 PM - 12:15 PM Registrations

​12:15 PM - 12:30 PM White Paper Presentation

​12:30 PM - 01:00 PM Panel: Market Opportunities to Enter Mexico: Cecilia Díaz, Managing Partner at AIMS Mexico

​01:00 PM - 01:30 PM Networking Buffet Lunch

​We are part of Toronto Tech Week, a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
