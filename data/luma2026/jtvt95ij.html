About Event

​Join digital health founders, product leaders, and marketers for a Harbourfront morning walk to connect, share ideas, and explore the future of health tech. Start your day with fresh air, meaningful conversations, and new industry connections at Toronto Tech Week. Move, connect, get inspired 👟

​Step away from the screen and into meaningful conversations with some of Toronto’s most forward-thinking voices in digital health. This relaxed, 1–hour morning walk is designed to spark ideas, build relationships, and create space for authentic connection – without the pressure of a formal event.

​Whether you’re building, scaling, or shaping the future of health, this is your chance to exchange perspectives with like-minded peers while enjoying one of the city’s best waterfront routes.

​☕ Coffee will be provided to kickstart your morning

​🚶 Light, guided group walk along Toronto’s Harbourfront

​🤝 Curated for digital health founders, product, and marketing leaders

​As a bonus, attendees can download the Optimity app to track their steps during the walk and earn rewards – adding a fun, interactive layer to the experience.

​Date: May 27

​Time: 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM

​Location: Waterfront Innovation Centre (Meet outside of building entrance)

​Come for the walk, stay for the conversations – and leave with new ideas, new connections, and a refreshed perspective.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
