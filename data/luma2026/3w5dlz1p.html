About Event

​Join us to hear from the 14 startups in the 2026 Google for Startups Accelerator: Canada cohort. They will each present their impactful AI/ML- driven businesses and share their inspiring journeys. Get ready to be inspired! We hope to see you there!

​Important: Due to limited in-person capacity, priority registration is reserved for investors, funds, and ecosystem partners, while all other guests are encouraged to register for the livestream event.

​​About the program: 
Google for Startups Accelerator: Canada is a 10-week hybrid accelerator program for Seed to Series A technology startups headquartered in Canada. The accelerator is designed to bring the best of Google’s products, people and technology to startups across the country. Learn more here. 

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech  torontotechweek.com.
