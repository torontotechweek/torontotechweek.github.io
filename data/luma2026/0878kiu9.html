About Event

​As Toronto’s tech ecosystem converges this May, Trndzy is opening its doors:

Real Talk. Real Trust. Real Discovery. 🚀

​Tired of the endless scroll of "sponsored" ads and fake reviews? So are we.

​Join Trndzy during Toronto Tech Week for a look at the future of how we shop, share, and connect. We’re cutting through the algorithmic noise to build something better: Human Infrastructure.

​Trndzy is the community-vetted platform that turns digital social and e-commerce chaos into confident decisions and buys. We’re replacing all the noise with peer-vetted proof, creating a space where:

​·       Shoppers find certainty (no more guessing!).

​·       Creators find their true value.

​·       Brands find real margins.

​The goal? To make "discovery" feel human again.

​Experience the Movement

​This isn’t just a presentation, it’s an invitation to get involved. During the event, we will host a live interactive session where you can:

  • ​Connect with a community of shoppers, founders, creators, and brands who are tired of the status quo.

  • ​Experience the app firsthand and see how we’re making discovery feel human again.

  • ​Collaborate on building a global infrastructure of trust.

​Whether you’re a shopper, a creator, a brand, a founder or just someone tired of the "pay-to-play" internet, come hang out with us. Let’s cancel the noise and build a global infrastructure of trust, together.

PS: Obsessed with beauty and wellness ? This event is made for you. Don't miss out!

​See you there! ✨

Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
