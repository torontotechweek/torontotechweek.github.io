About Event

​Presented by The AI Collective

​Ontario Hackathons, Yours to Discover

​We want Ontario to be known internationally for its hackathons.

​Within Ontario, we want young students to aspire to compete in hackathons, not just hockey.

​The way we get there is simple:

​More. Bigger. Better hackathons.

​

​We elevate the incredible hackathons that already exist.

​We encourage new hackathons to form.

​We learn from the best organizers across the province.

​

​🚀 Toronto Tech Week 2026: Hackathon Mixer

​For Toronto Tech Week 2026, we are starting with a Hackathon Mixer.

​This event brings together organizers and top hackers from across Ontario including communities such as:

​• KingHacks

​• UofT Hacks

​• GenAI Genesis

​• TMU Tech Week

​• CTRL+HACK+DEL

​• and more

​

​📍 Toronto

​📅 Toronto Tech Week (May 25–29)

​⏱️ ~2–3 hour networking mixer

​

​🤝 What This Event Is

​This event creates a space for:

​• Hackathon organizers

​• Hackathon winners and experienced hackers

​• Sponsors and community leaders

​to connect, share insights, and strengthen the hackathon ecosystem in Ontario.

​

​Our goal is to start building a united community:

​The Ontario Hackathon Coalition.

​

​Together, this coalition can attract the world’s best sponsors and support more hackathons across the province.

​👉 Sign up here.

​🌎 Long-Term Vision

​Looking ahead to Toronto Tech Week 2027 or 2028, the Ontario Hackathon Coalition could organize something much bigger.

​The goal would be to host the world’s largest single-location hackathon at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre, featuring:

​• world-class sponsors

​• thousands of hackers

​• a $1M grand prize

​

​✨ Why This Matters

​Hackathons are where future founders, engineers, and innovators meet.

​If Ontario’s hackathons collaborate and support each other, we can build one of the strongest builder ecosystems in the world.

​

​Ontario will be Yours to Discover for Hackathon Culture. 🚀

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.

​As always, food and drink, engaging conversation, and incredible company will all be provided!

​​Please be advised: Unfortunately, space is very limited at these community events and we can not always accept everyone we would like to. If you are not accepted to this event, please keep applying! We appreciate your application tremendously and we are looking forward to seeing you at a future event very soon!

​The AI Collective is a global non-profit building the human layer for the AI era. We unite 200,000+ leaders, builders, and stakeholders across 100+ forums worldwide to democratize the frontier, build trust, and coordinate how society navigates the rapid acceleration of technological progress.
