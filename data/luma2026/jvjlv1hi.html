About Event

​If you've tried Cowork or any AI assistant and walked away thinking "this is fine, I guess," you already know the problem: the tools are powerful but the setup is empty. Every session starts cold, with no memory of who you are, what you make, or how you think.

​Sean Oulashin is Stan's Senior Video Editor a Creator (500K+ across platforms). He spent the last several months building "Thinkspace", a done-for-you Cowork setup specifically for Creators.

​In this 45-minute workshop, Sean will show you:

  • ​The two foundational files every Cowork workspace needs, and why most setups skip them

  • ​The exact interview process he uses to build a creator's identity file in under 20 minutes

  • ​How to wire Cowork into the tools creators already use (Notion, Granola, Slack, calendar, Drive)

  • ​Where Cowork breaks for creators specifically, and the workarounds he's tested

  • ​A live walkthrough of his own Thinkspace, so you can see what "good" looks like

​Who this is for

  • ​Creators publishing content who want AI to actually help, not generate slop

  • ​Solopreneurs and operators running their own brand or business

​You don't need to be technical to attend! Sean built Thinkspace specifically for non-engineers.
