About Event

​Calling all techies, the gauntlet has been thrown. Rat Race is taking over Toronto's legendary underground P.A.T.H. network on May 28th as part of TechWeek TO, and we want to see if you have what it takes to navigate the maze, outlast the competition, and claim the title of Rat Race TechTO champion. It's chaotic, it's fun, and yes, you will get lost at least once.

​
Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
