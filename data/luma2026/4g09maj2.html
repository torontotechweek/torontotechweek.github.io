Round of 16 Tournament

32 players will be divided into 16 teams. One VC and 1 Founder in each team.

There will be winning team yes!

Also there will be two courts for casual gaming, fun content shoot and conversations + networking at the lounge area.

Location - Just 2 kms from Union Station. (Register to see)

Pickle & Pitch TTW Edition mixes quick, punchy pitches with plenty of energy, laughs, and good vibes; think snacks, sharp ideas, and a lively room that keeps things moving.

​Show up ready to share a 60-second pitch or cheer on someone else, grab a drink, and stick around for casual conversation afterward. Whether you’re testing an idea or just soaking up the atmosphere, this is a great excuse to get out, meet new faces, and have some fun...just like the last year at TTW...
