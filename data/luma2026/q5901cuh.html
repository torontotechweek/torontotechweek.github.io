About Event

​As part of Toronto Tech Week, Somos Latinx in Tech is throwing a night dedicated to side projects and side hustles. Think websites, apps, tools, prototypes, and experiments people are building after hours and on weekends.

​Come to demo what you're building, or simply take it all in: try projects live, ask creators what worked (and what didn't), and see how ideas become real through vision to prototype to iteration.

​This is a community-first night focused on learning by doing and learning by watching, built around real builders sharing real work in progress.

​Expect a short fireside chat with Hector Crespo (former Head of CX & Design at RBCX), live demos with Q&A, hands-on demo tables, and plenty of time to meet other makers and future collaborators.

​Want to demo your project? Submit here: https://forms.gle/QxgVSRBJYnGFWdd6A

​Event schedule

​5:30 PM - Arrivals and networking 6:00 PM - Welcome message

​6:10 PM - Fireside chat with Hector Crespo

​6:40 PM - Side project demos

​7:40 PM - Demo tables and networking

​8:30 PM - Closing words + what's next

​We invite allies to join us at Somos Latinx in Tech events to celebrate, support, and uplift the Latinx tech community alongside us.

​About Somos Latinx in Tech

​Somos Latinx in Tech is a nonprofit organization committed to supporting Latinx professionals to thrive in tech. We host events and programs throughout Canada, encouraging learning, networking, mentorship, and sponsorship, helping them grow their careers and shape the future of tech.

​www.somoslatinxintech.com

​About Toronto Tech Week

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what's next. From May 25-29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech.
