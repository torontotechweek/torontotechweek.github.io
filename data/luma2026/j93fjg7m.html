About Event

​Three Communities, One Night Thursday, May 28, 2026 | Sweat and Tonic, Toronto

​CS In Focus, Ruffday Services, and Women of CS are coming together for one evening during Toronto Tech Week.

​The yoga class kicks off at 6:30 PM — register for the "Guided Sauna Flow" class directly at sweatandtonic.com to secure your spot. After the 50-min class, we move to Tonic Bar for drinks and conversation.

​No panels. No pitches. Just three communities in one room, exchanging honest notes on what's happening in CS and tech right now.

​This evening is for Customer Success, GTM, and revenue leaders who are interested in post-sales.

​Spots are limited. This is intentionally small.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what's next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com

​

​About the hosts

​CS In Focus A community for Customer Success professionals to connect, learn, and have honest conversations about how the industry is evolving.

​Ruffday Services A mentorship and coaching platform for CS and GTM professionals navigating their next career move — from breaking into Customer Success to stepping into leadership.

​Women of Customer Success The leading global community for women in Customer Success and post-sales roles — building the next generation of CS leaders.
