About Event

​89% of women feel dismissed by their healthcare providers. Half the population. Centuries of data gaps. One question: what happens when AI finally listens?

​Join HerSay for an evening of bold conversation, community, and connection at the intersection of women's health and technology. Featuring a keynote from HerSay Founder & CEO Rebecca Lyons, a curated panel of femtech founders, clinicians, and investors, and an open networking hour with a live app demo — this is the room where the women building the future of health come together.

​This is not a panel about women. It's a panel by them.

​Drinks, dialogue, and a demo. Come ready to connect.
