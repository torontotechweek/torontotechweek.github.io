About Event

​A table tennis tournament and executive mixer during Toronto Tech Week.

We’re inviting leaders and innovators from Ontario’s best scaling tech companies to One King West’s Chairman’s Boardroom for an evening of bracket style table tennis, great drinks, canapés, and the kind of conversation that only happens when you put the right people in the right room.

Think Wes Anderson directs a founder soirée. No panels, no pitches, no lanyards. Wood panelled walls, a curated guest list, and a quick-match tournament threaded through the evening.

This is a curated guest list. If you’re here, someone thought you should be.

Dress code: Preppy casual. Like you wandered out of a Wes Anderson film and into a boardroom.

Hosted by Convictional.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
