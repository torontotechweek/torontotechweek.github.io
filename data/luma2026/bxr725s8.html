About Event

​AI is accelerating but team performance is not.

​As organizations adopt new tools and technologies, many are running into the same challenges: unclear ownership, slower decision-making, and growing disconnects between strategy and execution.

​This session brings together experienced leaders to unpack what it actually takes for teams to perform in the age of AI. This is not a passive panel, it is a guided, interactive conversation.

​In this session, we will explore: Why teams still matter in an AI-driven world The core attributes of high-performing teams The most common breakdowns teams experience today How leaders can improve decision-making, alignment, and trust. The discussion will go beyond surface-level insights.

​Panelists will challenge assumptions, share real experiences, and work through practical scenarios that leaders are facing today.

​Attendees will leave with clearer thinking, sharper questions, and a better understanding of how to strengthen their teams in an AI-driven environment.

Part of the “How Great Teams Win in the Age of AI” Roadshow

​This session is one stop in a multi-city experience bringing together leaders across North America to rethink how teams perform in an AI-driven world.

​Join us in a format that works best for you:

  • ​Boston (Virtual): May 28 at 2:00 PM

  • ​New York City (In-Person): June 3

  • ​SF: TBA

  • ​LA: TBA

​Each session offers a slightly different experience, but all are grounded in the same core idea: The next competitive advantage isn’t AI alone, it’s how your team performs with it.

​We’d love to see you at one (or more) of the stops.
