About Event

​Today we're opening our Investment Meeting to guests and those considering the Angel investment journey! Please join Maple Leaf Angels for our monthly Investment Meeting, followed by a Q+A with our investor members for those interested in becoming an Angel investor. Angel investing is exciting and effective way to support the Canadian startup ecosystem. Maple Leaf Angels provides early-stage founders with exposure to investors who wish to support with capital, expertise and mentorship.

​🤔 What does it actually feel like to back the next great Canadian startup? Come to a live Maple Leaf Angels Investment Meeting: watch real founders pitch, hear what experienced investors ask, and decide if this world is for you.

​❓ Not sure if you qualify to be an Angel investor? Head to mapleleafangels.com/investors to read more - requirements are at the bottom of the page.

​🏙️ Hosted at the stunning WeirFoulds office overlooking the city skyline. WeirFoulds LLP is a leading Canadian law firm known for trusted counsel to businesses, entrepreneurs, and growth-stage companies.

​🎓 After the pitches, we open the floor. Ask our members anything: why they joined, what they've learned, what they wish they knew sooner. If you've been curious about angel investing, this is the room to be in.

​This event is a proud part of Toronto Tech Week:
Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 26–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.
