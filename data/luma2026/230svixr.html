About Event

​As part of Toronto Tech Week 2026, join us for Hackathon Demo Day — a virtual showcase featuring the top 8 finalist teams and the innovative solutions they’ve brought to life through our AI-themed hackathon.

​Over the course of the hackathon, participants have collaborated, built, and refined ideas designed to tackle real-world challenges using AI. Demo Day is their moment to present live, share their journey, and celebrate the creativity and momentum that came out of this experience.

​This event is especially meaningful as it highlights the innovation and potential of immigrant talent in tech. At Scale Without Borders, we’re proud to create spaces where immigrant professionals can build, be seen, and connect with real opportunities across Canada’s tech ecosystem.

​✨ What to Expect

  • ​Live demos from the top 8 finalist teams

  • ​Inspiring speakers and community voices

  • ​A chance to discover emerging AI-driven solutions and bold ideas

  • ​A high-energy virtual showcase during Toronto Tech Week

​These finalists have put in the work — and this is their moment to shine. Come support them and see what’s possible when talent, community, and innovation come together.
