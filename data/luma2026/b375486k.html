About Event

​Built Here. Felt Everywhere.

​Toronto engineers are shipping technology used by millions of people. On May 28th, we're sitting down with some of the best and brightest to discuss honestly about what that’s like from the inside.

​Built Here. Felt Everywhere. is an evening in two parts: a live panel conversation followed by an immersive experience, hosted by Intuit at Arcadia Earth at The Well.

​6:30PM - BUILT HERE. SCALED EVERYWHERE.​
​
A candid conversation about what it takes to build technology that spans users around the globe—and what doing that from Toronto looks like.

​Moderated by April Hicke, Founder of TOAST Women in Tech, and featuring some of the most impressive builders from companies like Intuit, Warner Music & PwC.

​7:30PM - THE EXPERIENCE​
​
Step into Arcadia Earth's immersive projection environment, showcasing Toronto and the builder community that makes it. Let yourself mix, mingle, and discover what can be found. Afterwards, explore Arcadia Earth’s exhibit to get inspired for what the future may hold.

​When registering, share what you’re building and you may find yourself included in the experience.

​Builder Submission Link: https://forms.gle/WPEZpH59QNRws2B2A

​PLUS: DJ Sophie Jones, drinks and delicious bites, and complimentary access to the Arcadia Earth exhibits.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
