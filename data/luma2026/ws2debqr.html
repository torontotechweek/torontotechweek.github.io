About Event

​AI is everywhere - but selling physical products online is still full of friction.

​From unclear product visualization to endless content needs and high return rates, the core experience of buying physical goods hasn’t evolved as fast as the technology around it.

​This is a small, curated roundtable for founders, operators, and product leaders to explore what’s actually changing—and what’s not.

​Together, we’ll unpack:
• Where AI is creating real impact in commerce
• Why core problems like conversion and returns still persist
• What the next generation of product experience might look like

​No panels. No presentations. Just an honest, high-signal conversation with people building and operating in the space.

​Space is intentionally limited to keep the discussion thoughtful and engaging.
