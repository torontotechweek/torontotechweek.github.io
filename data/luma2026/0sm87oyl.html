About Event

​Toronto's best AI builders are taking the stage and we want you in the room.

​Apply to Demo — submissions close May 8th at 11:59 PM EST.

​Clio is partnering with Toronto Ruby to showcase the cutting edge of the Ruby AI ecosystem. This is an event for the engineers, the builders, and the innovators who are shaping what development looks like in the age of AI.

​As the global leader in legal AI, trusted by over 400,000 legal professionals worldwide, Clio built the Intelligent Legal Work Platform — a system of action powered by the world's largest legal data library, designed for a profession where accuracy and real outcomes aren't optional. That standard runs through everything we build. AI shapes how our engineers write, ship, and scale code every day.

​We’re opening our doors to the engineers who are holding themselves to that same high bar of craftsmanship.

​The Format: Pure Technical Showcase

​We’re looking for 10 of the most innovative builders to take the stage for a live technical showcase.

  • ​Show the Stack: Show us your IDE, your terminal, or your architectural diagrams.

  • ​The Q&A: After each 5-minute demo, our Engineering Leadership will step in with a question—asking the deep, technical questions about scale, latency, and trade-offs that only a peer would ask.

​The Run of Show

  • ​5:00 PM - Doors Open: Registration, food/beverages, and networking.

  • ​5:45 PM - Kickoff: Opening remarks from Clio’s CTO and a welcome from Toronto Ruby.

  • ​6:00 PM - Demos: Rapid-fire technical showcases with live Q&A from Clio’s Engineering Leadership.

  • ​7:00 PM - Open Floor: Connect directly with our Engineering and Product leadership, the Toronto Ruby community, and your fellow innovators.

​Want to Demo?

​Applications close May 8th. If you’ve built something that pushes the boundaries of Ruby and AI, whether it’s a custom LLM bridge, a unique agentic workflow, or a breakthrough in Ruby-based data processing, we want to see it. Apply to Demo

​Want to attend? Space is limited so RSVP via Luma today, to secure your spot in the room where the real building happens.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com
