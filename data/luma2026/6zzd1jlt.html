About Event

​When Worlds Converge: Art, AI & the Future of Human-Centered Innovation.

​Co‑Hosted by Schulich Office of Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Pursuits Inc. and Demo Room!

​Event Overview

​Schulich’s Office of Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Pursuits Inc, and Demo Room present a flagship event exploring the intersection of art, artificial intelligence, biometrics, and digital trust.

​The first public announcement and advance look inside the Heart Space Canadian exhibition tour (2027), a groundbreaking immersive digital artwork by internationally acclaimed artist Krista Kim, as she returns home following years of international exhibitions in Hong Kong, London, Barcelona, Seoul, Dubai, Las Vegas, New York, and more.

​Heart Space is the world’s first collaborative, AI-generative immersive artwork driven by real-time biometric data. Participants’ heartbeats (their unique biometric imprint) are translated into dynamic colours and waveforms, creating a continuously evolving collective composition…

​It is also an opportunity to understand the groundbreaking technology of Tenbeo (a Brussels-based biotechnology company pioneering identity authentication through advanced biometric AI) and technology provider behind the exhibition.

​As AI advances, protecting digital identities and proof-of-humanity protocols are key to safeguarding human agency, creativity, and dignity…

​Join us to hear Krista Kim and an amazing panel of arts and technology leaders explore these crucial concepts and delve into "proof-of-humanity" in the age of Artificial Intelligence.

​Toronto Tech Week is a citywide celebration of the people building what’s next. From May 25–29, 2026, founders, investors, and builders come together for hundreds of community-led events across Toronto, connecting tens of thousands of people around Canadian tech. Torontotechweek.com.

​ Meet the Artist

​Krista Kim is a Canadian-Korean artist known for her exploration of digital consciousness. Her work investigates the impact of digital technology on human perception, communication, and social structures. Through her practice, Kim pioneers a new language of calm and clarity for the digital age, with career highlights that include:

  • ​Cultural Leader of the World Economic Forum

  • ​Recognized by UNESCO as one of '50 Minds for the Next 50’

  • ​2025 Infinity Festival Monolith Awards - Fine Art

  • ​2024 & 2025 Excellence in Digital Art Award, Createurs Design Awards, Paris

  • ​Member of the Oxford Society of Aging and Longevity

  • ​First female NFT artist collected by LACMA

  • ​Exhibited at Hermitage State Museum, Times Square, the Sphere Las Vegas, and Davos 2024 (World Economic Forum)

  • ​Metaverse Editor for Vogue Singapore

  • ​Initiated the Techism Movement (2014), a philosophical framework recognizing technological innovation as an artistic discipline

​Meet the Technology Partner- Tenbeo

​Tenbeo is a Brussels-based biotechnology company pioneering identity authentication through advanced biometric AI. In collaboration with Krista Kim Studio, Tenbeo brings the Heart Space experience to life by integrating its cutting-edge heart wave recognition technology.

​Tenbeo’s technology uses a person’s distinct heart waveform to authenticate identity by analyzing real-time biometric data, ensuring only the true individual can access sensitive systems, prevent fraud, and secure digital transactions. Each person is made of unique electrical patterns.

​Details on the 2027 Exhibition 

​The full Heart Space Canadian Exhibition will launch in 2027 as part of a national tour. This 2026 event offers a rare first encounter with the work, and an opportunity to experience the vision of Krista Kim, the globally celebrated Canadian artist behind the work.
